# Copilot Creator Image Prompts — Wedding Botanical Assets

> Prompts for Microsoft Copilot Creator to generate watercolor botanical assets matching Cajsa & Filip's wedding invitations. Each prompt targets a specific asset needed for the website.

## Style Reference Summary

The invitations use a **soft watercolor** style with these characteristics:
- **Technique:** Wet-on-wet watercolor blending with soft, feathered edges — NOT hard outlines, NOT digital illustration
- **Color palette:** Muted sage/olive green leaves, dusty steel-blue accent flowers (1–2 per cluster), white/cream roses, small white filler flowers (baby's breath/gypsophila)
- **Leaf types:** Elongated eucalyptus leaves (silver dollar and willow varieties), heart-shaped eucalyptus populus leaves, small oval filler foliage
- **Flowers:** White/cream garden roses (dominant), dusty steel-blue anemones or garden roses (accent), tiny white gypsophila sprigs
- **Feel:** Delicate, translucent, hand-painted — visible paper texture through washes, leaves vary from dark olive (saturated) to pale sage (translucent wash)

## Tips for Copilot Creator

- Generate at **square or landscape** aspect ratio, then crop as needed
- Request **white background** (easier to remove in post-processing than a generated transparent background)
- Run each prompt **multiple times** and pick the best result — watercolor style varies between generations
- After downloading, use a background remover tool (e.g., remove.bg, Photoshop, GIMP) to get PNG with transparency

## Color Reference for All Prompts

When Copilot results come out with wrong tones, try adding these specific color callouts:

| Element | Color Description | Approximate Hex |
|---------|------------------|-----------------|
| Darkest leaves | Dark olive green | `#5C6B4F` |
| Standard leaves | Sage green | `#8FA07C` |
| Light leaf wash | Pale sage, almost transparent | `#B5C4A5` |
| Blue flowers | Dusty steel blue, muted | `#6B8BA4` |
| White roses | Warm ivory/cream, not pure white | `#F5F0E8` |
| Stems/branches | Warm brown-green | `#7A7A5A` |
| Baby's breath | Very pale cream/white | `#FAF8F5` |

## Post-Processing Checklist

After generating and selecting the best images:

1. **Remove background** — Use remove.bg, Photoshop, or GIMP to get transparent PNG
2. **Color-correct if needed** — Adjust hue/saturation to match the muted palette above (Copilot sometimes oversaturates greens or blues)
3. **Crop tightly** — Remove excess whitespace, but leave some natural breathing room around the edges of the foliage
4. **Export formats:**
   - PNG with transparency for the website (primary)
   - WebP with transparency as optimized version (smaller file size)
5. **Target file sizes:**
   - Large corner clusters: ~80–120 KB (WebP)
   - Small accents/dividers: ~30–50 KB (WebP)
   - Individual elements: ~15–25 KB (WebP)
6. **Resolution:** Generate at the highest available resolution; final assets should be at least 1000px on the longest side for large elements, 500px for small accents
